Services

What we can do for your home

Every service below is built on the same foundation: thorough preparation, quality materials, and care for the home we’re working in.

Interior

Interior Painting

Whether it’s one accent wall or a whole-home color change, interior work happens in your living space — so we treat it that way. Floors, furniture, and fixtures are protected before we start, surfaces are properly prepped, and we work cleanly and respectfully in occupied homes.

  • Walls, ceilings, trim, doors, and accent walls
  • Careful masking and protection of your home
  • Patching, sanding, and priming before paint
  • Crisp cut lines and smooth, durable finishes
Exterior

Exterior Painting

Western Washington weather is hard on paint. An exterior job that lasts here starts long before the first coat — with washing, scraping, sanding, and priming so the finish can actually grip and protect your siding and trim for years.

  • Siding, trim, doors, and entryways
  • Surface prep built for Pacific Northwest moisture
  • Quality, weather-resistant coatings
  • Protection for plants, decks, and walkways while we work
Cabinets

Cabinet Refinishing

Replacing cabinets is one of the most expensive parts of a kitchen or bathroom remodel. Refinishing gets you a fresh, updated look at a fraction of the cost — if the prep and coatings are done right. That’s the part we obsess over.

  • Kitchen and bathroom cabinets and vanities
  • Proper cleaning, sanding, and bonding primers
  • Durable coatings made for high-touch surfaces
  • A professional finish without full replacement
Supporting Work

Small Carpentry & Repairs

Paint can’t fix damaged wood. As part of a painting project we handle the small carpentry that makes the finish possible — repairing trim, patching damaged sections, and getting surfaces sound before they’re painted. (For major remodeling or structural work, you’ll want a general contractor — that’s not us.)

  • Minor trim repair and replacement
  • Small wood repairs on siding and fascia
  • Prep-related carpentry ahead of painting
