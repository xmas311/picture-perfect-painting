About Us

Welcome to Picture Perfect Painting, LLC, where we believe that a fresh coat of paint can transform not just walls, but entire spaces and lives. Founded in 2019, we are a proud small business rooted in the heart of Tumwater, Washington.

Our Story

Our journey began in 2000 when I moved to Olympia and instantly fell in love with this vibrant community. Growing up here, attending local schools, and eventually graduating from college in the area, I've developed a deep connection to the people and places that make Olympia special. This bond inspired me to establish Picture Perfect Painting, aiming to give back to the community that has given me so much.

Our Mission

At Picture Perfect Painting, we strive for perfection in every project we undertake. Our mission is to help our clients "Live Life in Full Color" by bringing their visions to life with precision and care. We understand that your home is your sanctuary, and we're here to enhance its beauty and functionality through expert craftsmanship.

Why Choose Us?

  • Community Commitment: As locals, we care deeply about our neighbors and take pride in contributing to the beauty of our community.
  • Personalized Service: We work closely with each client to understand their unique needs and preferences, ensuring a customized experience.
  • Passion for Excellence: Our passion drives us to exceed expectations and deliver results that stand the test of time.
