About Us

A local company with local roots

Owner-led, based in Tumwater, and painting Thurston County homes since 2019.

Our story

Hi, I’m Chris. I moved to Olympia in 2000 and grew up right here — local schools, local college, and a lot of years getting to know this community. When I started Picture Perfect Painting in 2019, it was partly a way to give back to the area that raised me, and partly because I genuinely enjoy helping people bring their ideas and visions to life.

I believe a fresh coat of paint can transform more than walls — it changes how a home feels. That’s where our slogan comes from: we want to help our clients Live Life in Full Color, with careful preparation, real craftsmanship, and personal service on every job.

Because this is a small, owner-led business, the person you talk to at the estimate is the same person looking after your project. No layers, no runaround — just direct communication and pride in the work.
